Condition Monitoring & Predictive Diagnostics

Preventing Plant Downtime Through Advanced Analytics

Unexpected equipment failures cost industrial plants millions in lost production. Our comprehensive condition monitoring program identifies micro-faults long before catastrophic breakdowns occur.

Our Diagnostic Methodologies

  • Infrared Thermography: Detecting thermal anomalies, loose electrical connections, and overloaded circuits.
  • Vibration Analysis: Isolating bearing wear, mechanical looseness, misalignment, and imbalance in rotating equipment.
  • Oil Analysis: Laboratory testing of lubricant condition, viscosity, and contamination wear particles.
  • Laser Shaft Alignment & On-Site Balancing: Restoring precision alignment to minimize mechanical stress and vibration.
